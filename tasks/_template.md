# Task Template

## Goal

What must be achieved and why it matters.

## Confirmed Facts

- Facts already established by docs, repository state, or explicit user direction.

## Assumptions

- Assumptions that are currently useful but not yet verified.

## Risks

- Risks that may affect delivery, correctness, scope or architecture.

## Deferred / Out of Scope

- What this task must not absorb.

## Open Questions

- Questions that require an explicit later decision.

## Next Decision

- The immediate decision needed to move the task forward.
